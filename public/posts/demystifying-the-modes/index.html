Demystifying the Modes

Playing modally is less complicated than you think

November 18, 2024

If you’re attempting to get to grips with modal playing, I’ll assume you’re able to play two octaves of any major scale. Ideally, you should be able to play every note across the entire fretboard for every major scale. This can be achieved with a series of shapes, not too dissimilar to the standard approach for learning the positions of the minor pentatonic scale.
